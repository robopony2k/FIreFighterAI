# Growth System

## Purpose
Handles passive ecological recovery, increasing fuel loads over time to simulate vegetation regrowth.

## Inputs
- Current fuel levels.
- **Static per-tile moisture (from map generation)**.
- Season data.
- Growth rate constants.

## Outputs
- Updated fuel per tile.
- Regrowth events (e.g., forest expansion).

## Key Tunables
- Base growth rate.
- Moisture multiplier.
- Seasonal modifiers (faster in growth phase).

## Performance Trade-Offs
- Simple per-tile updates (fast, O(n)).
- No spatial interactions (e.g., no competition models) for simplicity.
- Deterministic: Ensures reproducible maps.

## Notes on Moisture
- Moisture is treated as a long-term environmental modifier.
- It biases regrowth speed and success but does not change dynamically.
- Dynamic climate effects (e.g. hot summers) are handled separately and do not mutate base moisture.
